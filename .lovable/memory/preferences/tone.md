name: Tone and solution-based approach
description: All readings must use plain language and be solution-based with practical advice — never flowery, never negative without a solution
type: preference
---
ALL AI-generated content across every chamber must follow these rules:

**Plain language:**
- Simple, everyday words. Talk like a smart friend.
- Banned words: cosmic, mystical, ethereal, celestial, sovereign, divine, sacred, transcendent, alchemical, luminous, radiant, realm, vessel, channeling, resonance, vibration, awakening, veil, oracle, transmute, alchemy, tapestry, weave, unfurl, beckon.
- Short sentences. Get to the point.

**Solution-based (CRITICAL):**
- Every observation must come with practical, actionable advice.
- Never just name a problem — always decode it and give a real-world solution.
- Never say "today is a bad day" or "avoid X." Frame constructively: "This is a great day to slow down and plan."
- Frame challenges as opportunities with specific actions.
- Every reading leaves the person with something concrete they can DO.
- We DECODE — we give people tools to work with what they've got.

Applies to: generate-reading edge function, palm-reading edge function, fallbacks.ts, and any future content generation.
